The IEEE Information Theory Society (ITS) connects you to the latest research in its field of interest, which covers processing, transmission, storage, and information usage, and the theoretical and applied aspects of coding, communications, and communications networks. Membership includes online access to the Society Newsletter (print), IEEE Transactions on Informaton Theory (electronic), and the IEEE Information Theory Society Digital Library (electronic), as well as special rates on events such as the IEEE International Symposium on Information Theory, and access to its 3,000+ member professional network. Field of Interest (abridged): Processing, transmission, storage, and use of information; theoretical and applied aspects of coding, communications, and communications networks.

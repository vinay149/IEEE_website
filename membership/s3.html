Join the IEEE Education Society (EdSoc) and be part of a global community enhancing education within IEEE fields. The Society offers members affordable access to its peer-reviewed publications such as the IEEE Transactions on Education and sponsored events including the IEEE Global Engineering Education Conference (EDUCON). It also offers IEEE-RITA, a journal for authors writing in Spanish and-Portuguese. Field of Interest (abridged): Theory and practice of education and educational technology and professional development programs within IEEE fields.

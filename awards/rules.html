Outstanding Volunteer Awards Nomination Form And Rules For This Awards

Rules For This Aawards

1. Nominees must have served as volunteers.
2. Nominees must be currently active or must have volunteered within the past year.
3. Strong candidates have:
4. Positively influenced adult literacy in Minnesota.
5. Contributed unique, innovative or creative perspectives to adult literacy programs.
6. Shown dedication by the length of involvement or by a strong concentration of service.
7. Contributed above and beyond program requirements.
8. Demonstrated leadership ability.
9. ABE staff, MLC staff or board members, and former Outstanding Volunteer Award winners are ineligible.
10. Nominations are due by 5:00 p.m. on Monday, March 17, 2014.

Outstanding WIE student volunteer award Form And Rules For This Awards

Rules Of Awards

1. IEEE Region 10 WIE Student Award targets R10 WIE Student Member grade. IEEE Region 10 WIE Professional Award targets R10 WIE GOLD Member grade or higher.
2. Nominee must be an IEEE R10 WIE member in good standing who has made an identifiable creative achievement and/or a significant contribution toward the visions of R10 WIE.
3. Nominee must be an individual or a team (maximum two members per team).
4. The individuals nominated must be WIE members at the time of nomination.
5. Nominee's achievement and contribution must have been made within 3 years for this award.
6. A member of the R10 WIE committee cannot be Nominee.
7. Nominee must not have received the IEEE R10 WIE Volunteer (Most Inspiring Engineer, previously) Award within the last 3 years.

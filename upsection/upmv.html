IEEE MISSION&VISION OF UP IEEE

MISSION STATEMENT

To promote IEEE to prospective ,current students.
To influence others to pursue interest in technological innovation,
in the field of electrical and electronic engineering.
To develop and be able to share this interest,
turning this interest into inspiration for future generations

VISION STATEMENT

The IEEE Curtin University Student Branch will be one of the prominent societies on campus,
having developed a strong relationship with industry partners.
To be recognized as an active society hosting networking events that benefits students,
a society that provides volunteer work opportunity and career development.
